Frequently Asked Questions

Is account registration required?

Account registration with Dobi is necessary in order to keep transactions safe and, for the support plan customers, keep us in touch with you!

Can I hire you to install a product I bought somewhere else?

Yes! Absolutely! In these cases we can't offer any warranty for these products.

What is the currency used for all transactions?

We are working with Euro.

I'm building a new house, when should I contact you guys?

The sooner, the better! This way we can work our way to fit into your plans and we can even help you with design suggestions to improve (even more) your experience once the products are installed.

I already have some level of automation, would it be a problem having both systems?

Each case is an individual case and we have options about how to tackle this matter. We can integrate your system to ours with only one control center or, if you prefer, just add morre functionality to your pre-existing system

How much it costs in average to have your system installed?

There's no pre-fixed price as we literally build what you need. But you can fill this form and we can send you an estimative. You can always build your automation system in steps as your needs change, as you want more comfort and control or just because you want!

I live in Russia, how can I get your services?

If you have a plan, you can come to us (or buy online) and we can send to you the products with the schemes for installation. Unfortunately we still aren't offering our installation services in Russia.

What are the payment options?

You can pay through bank transference, credit/debit cart or paypal.

I have a huge garden and when I'm away there's no one to water my plants! Can you offer something to help me?

Of course we can! We have systems that can be controlled via internet so you can water your plants anytime, more or less depending on the weather for example.
