Why do I need automation?

Automation is the art of having a machine to do what a person would be doing. Usually boring and time-consuming activities as laundry, washing dishes or sometimes just normal activities that would require your presence like checking if the switches in the house are turned off, to feed your fish or water your plants when you're at work or on holidays.

Life is throwing at us an immense flow of challenges and information that can be overpowering when we can't keep it all under control. Dobi simplifies it by gathering all you need to control into one point. From a single device you can check and answer emails, if your heating is on, if the baby still asleep, change channels on your TV, change the lighting or even open up the curtains for the sun to come in.
